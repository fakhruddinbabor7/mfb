About Me

I'm currently pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at Washington State University under supervision of Dr. Monowar Hasan. Im broadly working on the field of cyber-physical system security and deploying Large Language Models in embedded device. I did my bachelor's in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) in 2017. After my bachelor's, I have 4.5 years of job experience in the industry. I love traveling, running and playing soccer.
