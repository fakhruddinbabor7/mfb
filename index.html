I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Washington State University, advised by Dr. Monowar Hasan . My work spans confidential deep-learning inference in trusted execution environments, information leakage in fixed-priority systems, randomized scheduling and control stability, embedded language-model performance, and learning-based cyber vulnerability prioritization. I did my bachelor's in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) in 2017. After my bachelor's, I have 4.5 years of job experience in the industry. I love traveling, running, and playing soccer.
